Hi! I'm Kenny, an computer engineer with a recent Master's degree in Logic, Computing, and Artificial Intelligence from the University of Seville. My academic background has focused on key areas such as Artificial Intelligence, data science, and exploring different programming paradigms.

Currently, I hold the position of specialist technician in the Department of Industrial Organization and Management of Companies II at the University of Seville. My work focuses on applied research of machine learning techniques, specifically aimed at improving Production Planning and Control in the metallurgical industry.

My technical experience encompasses significant projects in machine learning, using the Python programming language and its comprehensive set of tools for Machine Learning (Scikit-Learn, NumPy, pandas, matplotlib, PyTorch, Gradio, among others). Additionally, I have explored the field of functional programming, employing Haskell to develop parallel algorithms on GPUs.
